## ⚡ Study Strategy

1. **Week 1–2**: Domains 1 & 2 (Git basics + repos) — the foundation of everything
2. **Week 3**: Domains 3 & 4 (Collaboration + Actions/Copilot/Codespaces)
3. **Week 4**: Domains 5, 6 & 7 (Projects, Security, Community)
4. **Week 5**: Review + take all practice tests + boss fight quizzes in this repo
5. **Exam day**: You got this! 🎮
